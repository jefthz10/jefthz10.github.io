My Travel Experience and Adventures

Mariano's Blooming Petals

I've been here for a couple times but I did't have the chance to witness these sunflowers in full bloom.

Neri's Ville Selfie Corner

This tourist attraction has a plenty instagrammable spots like in this photo. A very nice, relaxing and creative place.

Philippine Military Academy

On September 12, 2018, I was able to set foot in this military academy.

Danao Church

My favorite old church in Northern Cebu.

Valley of Colors

I love this amazing place and it's amazing artworks. A lovely and colorful place to visit.

Florentino's Eco Park

Also known as the "Little Tagaytay of Cebu". It is a beautiful place to unwind and to do photoshoots. What I love the most in this place is the panoramic view of the mountain ranges and the fresh cozy ambiance.

Eden Nature Park & Resort

Eden Nature Park is not just a famous tourist destination in Davao but also my favorite place in Davao. I enjoyed the food and the breat-taking views while riding in this skyline bicycle. Whoaa

People's Park

I love the relaxing vibe in this place. This area is well-maintained and litter free. A lot of people goes here for relaxation and do excercises.

The Famous Lion's Head

You will pass this attraction when you enter and leave Baguio. You can also take pictures in their traditional Ifugao warriors clothes and could purchase snacks and souvenirs in this area.

10, 000 Roses Cafe

I had an amazing experience watching over the spectacular view of the 10, 000 roses especially in the night time because of the light and the refreshing sea breeze outside.

Mines View Park

I can't really forget this place because of its beautiful views and I also got the chance to wore their Ifugao traditional clothes.

Temple of Leah

The place is so amazing and the overlooking view from the City. I love the monuments, veranda and the Roman architecture vibes of the place. Definitely, one of the best place to visit when you travel to Cebu.

Sumilon Island

I had an awesome experience in this island. I really love the crystal clear water, the sandbar, serenity and calmness of this place. I and my friends had a great time and so much fun in this island.

The Mansion

The Mansion is the official summer residence of the President of the Philppines. It is a cool place but you can only take pictures in front of the Mansion.

Magellan's Cross

The historical landmark of Cebu. A very popular and historical place. This is one of my favorite attractions in Cebu since this place has a lot of story to be told especially in the history of the Philippines.

Sto. Nino de Basilica Minore

A beautiful place for worship and very historic place. Travel in Cebu will not be complete without coming in this old church with its old beautiful structures.

JVR Island in the Sky

I enjoyed crossing in this colorful hanging bridge while watching the beautiful sceneries in this area. When you come in the place early in morning you'll definitely love the weather, it gives you a vibe of Baguio's weather.

Cap John Hay

This place is very relaxing and cool. I love the ambiance and the countryside vibe of the place.

Fort San Pedro

This old famous church in Davao.

San Pedro Cathedral

Siok Falls

It is one of the eco-tourism destination in Koronadal, South Cotabato. I enjoyed the river trekking adventure and the fresh ambiance of the air in this place.

Cancu-Ay Beach Resort

This is the first place I've visited in South Cebu. I love the refreshing sceneries especially the rock formation and the clear water of the beach. I had so much fun taking photos in this beautiful beach.

Old Diplomat Hotel

For me, this is my top one favorite spot in Baguio. It is one of the of the most abandoned hotels in the world. I had a great time strolling inside the old diplomat hotel and what I love is the foggy weather that makes the hotel more spooky. I experience the most foggy weather in my entire life in this area. The cool breeze of the fogs gives me the vibe of excitement and amazing feeling.
